What We Believe

Daily life is immeasurably better—more convenient, efficient, engaging, and fulfilling—thanks to technology. It's through technology that we learn new skills, launch new businesses, and start new relationships. With the tap of an app or the click of a button, we can instantly reach therapists, doctors, caretakers, dog walkers, and chefs. These tools not only level the playing field for everyone, saving us time and money, but they foster real emotional connections and provide us with invaluable peace of mind. We recognize the passion and hard work that goes into these life-changing products, and we champion the people behind them.

We seek out bold, ambitious founders who share our optimism for consumer technology and are dedicated to leading that future-first vision into the next era. We believe in proving our value through action, not talk. We’ve helped companies navigate downturns and explosive growth, and we are committed to supporting founders through the ups and downs of the business cycle. Drawing on our extensive network and hard-earned experience, we aim to help founders not merely stand out from the competition, but break out—and win. We strive to back the next generation of builders making their mark on humanity.
