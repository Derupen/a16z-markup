Empowering the Individual

New technology companies are reinventing the way we work, play, eat, commute, learn, and socialize, and the a16z consumer tech team believes in—and bets on—these companies that are shaping our future. That translates to a deep respect for the entrepreneurial process and an avid interest in new (sometimes crazy-sounding) ideas. We’re intrigued by the next generation of video, audio, communication and social platforms, as well as apps that are fundamentally shaping the consumer experience in real estate and education.
